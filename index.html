The Person Behind the Work

Liane Menendez

Founder, The Lineage Studio

I started The Lineage Studio because I kept seeing the same thing — families with years of careful research and no way to share it. Photos in boxes. Stories never written down. Ancestors reduced to names on a chart that nobody ever reads.

I founded this studio to bridge that gap. Using over 20 years of hands-on genealogy experience, I transform what families have already gathered into something they can finally hold, share, and pass down.

Every project is handled personally, with care for both the history and the people it belongs to.
